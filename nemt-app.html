Platform Features

Every Tool Your Operation Needs

Built by NEMT industry veterans who understand the unique complexity of coordinating trips, managing compliance, and maximizing reimbursement at scale.

🗺️
Dispatch & Trip Management

Real-time dispatching with intelligent driver assignment, GPS tracking, and automated status updates for every trip in your fleet.

  • Drag-and-drop scheduling board
  • Live GPS with ETAs and geofencing alerts
  • Automated trip confirmation and reminders
  • Ride-share and multi-leg trip support
💳
Billing & Claims Automation

Integrated Medicaid, MCO, and broker billing workflows that maximize clean claim rates and accelerate reimbursement cycles.

  • Real-time eligibility verification
  • Auto-claim generation from completed trips
  • ERA/EOB posting and denial management
  • Multi-payer and multi-state support
🛡️
Compliance & Credentialing

Automated driver and vehicle credential tracking with expiration alerts, audit-ready documentation, and regulatory compliance monitoring.

  • Driver license, background & DMV tracking
  • Vehicle inspection and insurance expiration alerts
  • ADA compliance documentation
  • Audit-ready trip logs and reporting
📊
Analytics & Reporting

Custom dashboards that surface the KPIs that matter — from cost-per-mile and denial trends to driver performance and revenue forecasting.

  • Executive, operational, and financial dashboards
  • Denial trend analysis and root cause reporting
  • Driver efficiency and utilization metrics
  • Scheduled report delivery via email
🔗
Broker & Payer Integrations

Pre-built integrations with major NEMT brokers and Medicaid management systems for seamless trip imports, authorization, and claims submission.

  • Modivcare, MTM, LogistiCare connectivity
  • Medicaid MMIS and MCO connections
  • HL7 / FHIR healthcare data interoperability
  • Open API for custom integrations
📱
Driver Mobile App

A purpose-built mobile application for drivers that handles navigation, trip manifests, electronic signatures, and real-time communication.

  • iOS and Android support
  • Electronic signature and trip verification
  • In-app messaging and dispatch communication
  • Offline-capable trip management
